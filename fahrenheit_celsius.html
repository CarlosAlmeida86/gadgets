Welcome to our Fahrenheit to Celsius conversion tool! This website allows you to easily convert temperatures from Fahrenheit to Celsius or vice versa. Simply enter the temperature you want to convert in the appropriate field and press the "Convert" button to see the result. Whether you're traveling to a new country with a different temperature scale or just want to know how cold it is in Celsius, this tool has you covered. Give it a try and see how easy it is to convert between these units of measurement.
To convert temperatures in degrees Fahrenheit to Celsius, subtract 32 and multiply by .5556 (or 5/9).
Example: (50°F - 32) x 0.5556 = 10°C.
To convert temperatures in degrees Celsius to Fahrenheit, multiply by 1.8 (or 9/5) and add 32.
Example: (30°C x 1.8) + 32 = 86°F’.
